Clinic story

Designed around services, screening and continuity of care.

We built Ibotombi Eye Care to make consultation, diagnosis and treatment planning feel straightforward. The experience is service-first: visitors find out quickly what care is available, who it is relevant for, and how to request appointments.

Instead of template filler, every patient pathway — children's myopia, adult glaucoma, diabetic retina or pediatric therapy — is structured so your first visit already has a clear next step.

  • Comprehensive services for all age groups with ophthalmic concerns
  • Myopia screening for school-going children and follow-up pathways
  • Glaucoma care emphasis for adults above 50 and at-risk patients
  • Retina screening support for diabetic patients
Vision & mission

World-class eye care, delivered locally in Manipur.

Our commitments are not decorative. They shape how we screen, counsel, refer and follow up every patient.

Vision

To establish a centre of excellence in ophthalmology, deliver world-class eye care service and help eliminate avoidable blindness.

Mission

To deliver global-standard eye care and surgical excellence locally — with compassion, research, learning, advanced technology and respect for underserved communities.
