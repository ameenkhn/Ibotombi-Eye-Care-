Orthoptek Therapy

A newly added option for amblyopia and binocular vision support.

Orthoptek was installed at Ibotombi Eye Care on April 17, 2026. Based on the available published and clinical descriptions, it is a non-invasive treatment approach used in amblyopia and related binocular vision disorders through visual stimulation training.

The device has been described as aiming to stimulate parvocellular and magnocellular pathways and support improvement in monocular vision, stereopsis and binocular cooperation in selected patients. Suitability still depends on proper examination and clinical judgment.

  • Relevant for lazy eye / amblyopia discussions
  • Can be considered in selected binocular vision or stereopsis-related cases
  • Non-invasive therapy pathway
  • Integrated into the clinic's pediatric vision and therapy service mix
