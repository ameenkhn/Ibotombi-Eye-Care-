Before your visit

Three quick answers patients often ask.

Appointments

Do I have to book?

Walk-ins are welcome, but booking helps coordinate your visit faster and keeps waiting time short.

Records

What should I bring?

Previous prescriptions, reports, ongoing medication details and — for diabetic patients — recent sugar records.

Urgent

Can I come today?

For sudden vision loss, pain, trauma or severe redness — call immediately. Same-day review is prioritised.
