What services does Ibotombi Eye Care provide?

We provide cataract, cornea, glaucoma, medical retina, oculoplasty, dry eye management, myopia management, amblyopia management, vision therapy, contact lens care, cornea crosslinking and Orthoptek-based therapy. Each service includes a clear consultation pathway and structured follow-up.

Do you treat children as well as adults?

Yes. We treat all age groups with eye problems and maintain a dedicated focus on school-going children who need myopia screening and regular follow-up. Pediatric vision therapy is a core part of our practice.

Who should come in for glaucoma screening?

Adults above 50 years and patients with glaucoma risk factors — such as a family history or diabetes — should consider timely review. Glaucoma can progress quietly without obvious symptoms, so routine checks are vital.

Why is diabetic retina screening important?

Diabetic patients can develop retinal changes before they notice any symptoms. A dedicated retina screening helps identify issues early and supports safer long-term vision outcomes.

Can I walk in, or do I need to book first?

We continue to support walk-in check-ins. However, calling the clinic, using the website form or sending a WhatsApp request helps us coordinate your visit faster and keeps waiting times predictable.

How does online booking work on this website?

The contact page form collects your details and opens WhatsApp with a structured, pre-filled message addressed to the clinic. That keeps online booking functional right now without waiting for a full backend system.

What are the clinic timings?

Consultation timings are Monday to Saturday from 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM, and Sunday from 7:30 AM to 12 PM. Early morning slots work well for school children and working adults.

Do you provide contact lens support?

Yes. The clinic provides contact lens selection, fitting support and day-to-day usage guidance as part of its optical care offering — comfort, hygiene and long-term wear are all reviewed.

What is Orthoptek and who is it for?

Orthoptek is a non-invasive therapy device installed at the clinic on April 17, 2026, used for selected amblyopia (lazy eye) and binocular vision related cases. Suitability is confirmed only after a proper clinical examination.

When should I seek urgent eye evaluation?

Sudden pain, redness, trauma, rapid vision loss, severe blurring or symptoms after an injury should never be ignored. Call the clinic immediately for guidance — early review prevents permanent damage in many cases.

Where exactly is the clinic located?

Ibotombi Eye Care is in Canchipur, Langthabal Kunja, south of Manipur University Main Gate, Imphal West, Manipur 795003. You will find a map and directions on the Contact page.

Do you offer follow-up care?

Yes. Follow-up is structured into every patient pathway — from post-cataract review to glaucoma monitoring, pediatric myopia tracking and diabetic retina screening over time.
